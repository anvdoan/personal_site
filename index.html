About Me

Hi! My name is An Vinh Doan (I go by An!) and I’m currently a junior at the University of Michigan. I am a dual-degree student studying Information through the School of Information and Gender & Health through the College of Literature, Science, and the Arts (LSA). I’m originally from Grand Rapids, Michigan, so I’m a born and raised Michigander. My goal throughout my coursework and career is to combine my interests in technology, social justice, and health to address and analyze health disparities in marginalized groups and create technology-assisted solutions. If you happen to find me outside of classes or work, I’ll probably be cooking, trying new skincare routines, or catching up on new TV shows, movies, and books.
