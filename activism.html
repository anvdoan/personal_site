Activism

SAPAC

I’ve been a student volunteer with the Sexual Assault Prevention and Awareness Center (SAPAC) since my freshman year at U-M. I am a part of the Survivor Empowerment and Ally Support (SEAS) program where I’ve worked on creating a workshop about responding to disclosures of sexual assault. I have also facilitated many discussions surrounding topics, such as restorative justice, effective allyship, issues faced by sex workers, and trauma-informed sexuality.

Cryptoparty Ann Arbor

I am a part of Cryptoparty, which is a group based in Ann Arbor that focuses on educating the community about digital privacy and security and promoting community technology principles. We are also focusing on passing a facial recognition ban in Ann Arbor and you can find the petition here. You also can feel free to check out our website.
